Roots is an enterprise-sponsored community garden project in Ireland. Our mission is to make Ireland the best place to live and work by engaging with local communities and fostering a sense of belonging through community gardening activities, changing unused lands into fostering gardens, with sponsorship and helps from companies and other institutions.
